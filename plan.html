Plan Your Trip

The wedding will take place at The Lumberyard in Huntsville, Alabama.

Flights

Huntsville has an airport (HSV), so it might be best to check flights there first. Flying into Huntsville could eliminate the need to rent a car (there are hotel shuttles and plenty of Ubers in the area). If flights to Huntsville don't work, Nashville (BNA) is another good option. It's about a 2 hour drive from Nashville to Huntsville.



Hotels

We're recommending The Embassy Suites ($149/night) or The SpringHill Suites Huntsville ($104/night) as they are both about a mile from the venue. Make sure to use the links here for the group rate. Alternatively, there are a good amount of hotels in downtown Huntsville so feel free to shop around or grab an Airbnb with a group.

Things to do in Huntsville

U.S. Space and Rocket Center

That's right, Huntsville aka "Rocket City" is the home of the NASA Space and Rocket Center (and SPACE CAMP). The museum has one of the most extensive collections of space artifacts and displays more than 1500 pieces. Displays include rockets, engines, spacecraft, simulators, and hands-on exhibits. We will be having a Sunday brunch at the Space and Rocket center if you want to check it out!

Monte Sano State Park

If space isn't your jam, you could go for a hike, bike ride, or play some disc golf in Monte Sano State Park. Monte Sano is a small mountain that overlooks Huntsville. Cool views and waterfalls and nature stuff!

Huntsville Top Golf

If you haven't been to a Top Golf before, now is your chance. If you're what they call a "ball golfer" and think you can drive further than your friends/family, convince them to go with you. Reminder: the "I woulda beat you if I had my own clubs" excuse is played out..

Monster Jam?!

Holy cannoli, we were lucky enough to pick the same weekend as MONSTER JAM for our wedding weekend! The monster truck shows are at 1pm Saturday and Sunday in Huntsville (the wedding is at 5pm on Saturday, please don't be late because of 'monster jam'). If you're feeling extra extreme you could go scope the trucks sporting your finest wedding attire (if anyone does this, send pics). Think our 4Runner could hang with the monsters?! Well, she's a beast to us.

Beer

If space, golf, trucks, and mountains weren't enough for you, there's always beer. With 8 craft breweries (and growing), the Greater Huntsville area is one of the Craft Beer Capitals of the South. Downtown Huntsville is a really nice place to take a stroll, eat some southern BBQ, and have a few brews.

  • Church Street Wine Shoppe
  • Green Bus Brewing
  • Liquor Express and Craft Beer Store

  • Salty Nut Brewery
