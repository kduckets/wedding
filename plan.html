Plan Your Trip

The wedding will take place at The Lumberyard in Huntsville, Alabama.

Flights

Huntsville has an airport (HSV), so it might be best to check flights there first. Flying into Huntsville could eliminate the need to rent a car, as there are hotel shuttles and plenty of Ubers in the area. If flights to Huntsville don't work, Nashville (BNA) is another option. It's about a 2 hour drive from Nashville to Huntsville.



Hotels

We're recommending The Embassy Suites ($149/night) or The SpringHill Suites Huntsville ($104/night) as they are both about a mile from the venue. Make sure to use the links here for the group rate. There are a good amount of hotels in downtown Huntsville so feel free to shop around or grab an Airbnb with a group.

Things to do in Huntsville

U.S. Space and Rocket Center

That's right, Huntsville aka "Rocket City" is the home of the NASA Space and Rocket Center (and SPACE CAMP). The museum has one of the most extensive collections of space artifacts and displays more than 1500 pieces. Displays include rockets, engines, spacecraft, simulators, and hands-on exhibits. Highly recommended by the nerds getting married.

Monte Sano State Park

If space isn't your thing you could go for a hike, bike ride, or play some disc golf in Monte Sano State Park. Monte Sano is a small mountain that overlooks Huntsville. Cool views and waterfalls and nature stuff!
